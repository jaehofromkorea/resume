Cover Letter

Dear Sir or Madam,

Seeking professional experience in France, I am submitting my application for a position as a Chef.

Education & Background

Having obtained a culinary arts diploma from a high school in Seoul, I worked as a kitchen assistant while completing my studies. After my military service, where I also worked in the kitchen, I decided to move to Australia to further develop my culinary skills. There, I had the opportunity to work at prestigious establishments such as the Sydney Opera House. I deliberately chose to work at various restaurants to diversify my knowledge and techniques as a chef.

What I Learned

Through these numerous professional experiences, I was able to apply my skills while demonstrating strong adaptability. Working in different restaurants allowed me to deepen my understanding of the profession. I had the chance to observe and learn from the unique strengths of each chef I worked with.

Achievements

As a chef, I have had the opportunity to take on various responsibilities. With a particular passion for plating and presentation, I was entrusted with this aspect in several restaurants where I worked.

Personal Qualities

Dynamic, motivated, and dedicated, I would like to express my sincere enthusiasm and willingness to contribute my skills as a chef to your team. I value teamwork and am confident in my ability to quickly integrate and collaborate effectively with your staff. I am ready to fully commit to any responsibilities entrusted to me.

Thank you for considering my application. I remain available for any additional information or for an interview at your convenience. Please find attached a gallery showcasing various dishes I have created throughout my career.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours sincerely,
